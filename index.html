About Me

I am a Machine Learning Engineer with a strong passion for artificial intelligence and computer vision. Originally from India, I embarked on my academic journey by pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, where I honed my programming and technology skills. This solid foundation paved the way for my pursuit of a Master's degree with a specialization in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence at the esteemed Saarland University in Germany.

My expertise shines in the field of computer vision, where I leverage advanced techniques such as the YOLOv7 model. A notable project that showcases my skills is an innovative application designed for gym environments. This application provides real-time feedback on posture correction, demonstrating how I combine technical acumen with practical applications to enhance health and fitness routines.

With a strong command of computer vision and machine learning, along with my comprehensive educational background, I am dedicated to exploring new technological advancements. My goal is to continue contributing meaningful solutions to the AI landscape, driven by a blend of creativity, technical expertise, and a keen understanding of user needs.
